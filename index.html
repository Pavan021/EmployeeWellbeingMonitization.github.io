About the tool!
This Employee Wellbeing monetization tool was developed by NYU Stern Center for Sustainable Business in collaboration with our apparel company partners using our Return on Sustainability Investment (ROSI™) methodology. This tool can be used to monetize companies' investments in employee wellbeing strategies across industries. We welcome your feedback.
