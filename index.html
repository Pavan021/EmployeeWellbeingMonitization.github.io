  • Understanding Your ROSI Estimate:

    • Your result is a directional estimate based on CSB's work with clients across industries,as well as academic research.A key assumption to this model is that by investing in employee well-being initiatives,companies will be able to close the gap between industry level turnover and their own turnover and increase the productivity of top performers.

    • "Well-being Initiatives" generally refers to sustainability initiatives that impact the entire employee base through PTO policies,involvement in Sustainability Green Teams in the workplace, etc.

  • Next Steps

    • ROSI Talent Attraction,Productivity,and Retention Model
